Our Mission



PLA is a network built to give pre-law students across Philadelphia a space to connect, compare notes, and figure out what a career in law actually looks like without having to join mock trial to find your people.

Why We Exist


- Mock trial is one of the only ways to meet students from other Philly schools, and it's not for everyone.


- Law is often treated as the default path. We want to clarify what that actually means.


- We're pre-law students too, figuring this out alongside you.
